    The Rules

    Game Flow

    • The contestant must answer 15 questions (in Germany) with increasing difficulty.
      •
    • Each question has four answer choices (A, B, C, D), with one correct answer.
      •
    • With each correct answer, the prize money increases.
      •

    Prize Tiers & Safety Levels

    • Safety Level 1: €1,000 (at question 5)
      •
    • Safety Level 2: €16,000 (at question 10)
      •
    • If the contestant reaches a safety level, this amount is guaranteed in case of an incorrect answer attempt.
      •
