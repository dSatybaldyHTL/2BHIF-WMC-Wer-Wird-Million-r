    The Rules

    Gameplay


    • The contestant must answer up to 15 questions to win £1,000,000.
    • There are four possible answers, only one of which is correct.
    • Each correct answer increases the winnings.
    • Questions become harder with each step.
    • There is no time limit; the contestant can quit at any time.
    • A wrong answer drops the contestant to the last guaranteed amount.
    • The game starts easy and becomes progressively harder.

    Prize Levels & Safety Nets

    • Safety Net 1: £1,000 (at question 5)
    • Safety Net 2: £32,000 (at question 10)
    • Reaching a safety net ensures the contestant keeps that amount even after a wrong answer.

    Lifelines

    • 50:50: Two incorrect answers are removed.
    • Phone a Friend: The contestant calls a friend for advice (30 seconds).
    • Ask the Audience: The audience provides a percentage-based opinion.
    • Ask the Host: The host gives a personal opinion.
    • Switch the Question: The contestant can skip the question and receive a new one.

    Contestant's Choices

    • Select and confirm an answer; no changes allowed.
    • Use a lifeline for assistance.
    • Quit the game and take the winnings.
    • Take a risk and continue playing. A wrong answer drops them to the last safety net.

    Rule Variations

    • Shorter version: Sometimes only 12 questions with adjusted prize levels.
    • "Fastest Finger First": Previously, contestants had to answer a qualifying question to enter the game.
    • Time limit: Some versions had a set time per answer.
    • Special editions: Celebrity or themed episodes.
