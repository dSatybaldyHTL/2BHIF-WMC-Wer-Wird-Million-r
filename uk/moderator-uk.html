    The Host

    Who is Chris Tarrant?

    (The first host of the UK version)

    Chris Tarrant is a British television presenter best known for hosting the quiz show Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? He presented the show from 1998 to 2014 and shaped the format with his distinctive style, which was adapted worldwide.


    Early Life and Education

    Chris Tarrant was born on October 10, 1946, in Reading, England. He attended King’s School in Worcester and showed an early interest in journalism and entertainment. He later studied English at the University of Birmingham and began his career in radio.


    Career

    Chris Tarrant started his career in broadcasting and television in the 1970s. Some key milestones in his career include:

    • 1972–1983: Host of the children's show Tiswas
    • 1984–1992: Host of the show OTT
    • 1993–2004: Presenter of the radio show Capital Breakfast
    • 1998–2014: Host of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?

    Tarrant became known for his humorous and suspenseful hosting style. His catchphrase "Is that your final answer?" became famous worldwide.


    Personal Life

    Chris Tarrant has been married twice and has several children. Besides his television career, he is involved in various charitable organizations. In 2014, he suffered a stroke but recovered well.

    Who is Jeremy Clarkson?

    (Current host of the UK version)

    Jeremy Clarkson is a British television presenter, journalist, and author best known internationally for the automotive show Top Gear. Since 2018, he has hosted the revival of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?, bringing his signature humor to the show.


    Early Life and Education

    Jeremy Clarkson was born on April 11, 1960, in Doncaster, England. He attended Repton School, where he was known for his rebellious behavior. After school, he pursued a career in journalism, specializing in automotive journalism.


    Career

    Clarkson’s career spans several decades in television and print media. Key milestones include:

    • 1988–2015: Lead presenter of the BBC show Top Gear
    • Since 2016: Presenter of the Amazon show The Grand Tour
    • Since 2018: Host of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?

    Clarkson adds his signature sarcasm and sharp-witted comments to the quiz show, giving it a fresh dynamic.


    Personal Life

    Jeremy Clarkson has been married twice and has three children. He lives on a farm in the Cotswolds and has been running his own farm since 2021, which is documented in the series Clarkson’s Farm.
