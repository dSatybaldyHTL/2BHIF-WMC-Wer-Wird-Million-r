Who Wants to Be a Millionaire

The Host

Who is Chris Tarrant?

(The first UK host)

Chris Tarrant is a British television presenter, best known for hosting the quiz show Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? He presented the show from 1998 to 2014 and became synonymous with its distinctive format, which was adapted worldwide.


Early Life and Education

Chris Tarrant was born on October 10, 1946, in Reading, England. He attended King’s School in Worcester and showed an early interest in journalism and entertainment. He later studied English at the University of Birmingham and began his career in radio.


Career

Chris Tarrant’s broadcasting career started in the 1970s. Key milestones include:

  • 1972–1983: Host of children’s show Tiswas
  • 1984–1992: Presenter of the show OTT
  • 1993–2004: Host of Capital Breakfast radio show
  • 1998–2014: Host of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?

Tarrant became famous for his suspenseful and humorous presenting style. His catchphrase "Is that your final answer?" is recognized worldwide.


Personal Life

Chris Tarrant has been married twice and has several children. Besides his television career, he is involved in various charitable organizations. In 2014, he suffered a stroke but made a full recovery.

Who is Jeremy Clarkson?

(Current UK host)

Jeremy Clarkson is a British television presenter, journalist, and writer, best known for the automotive show Top Gear. Since 2018, he has hosted the revival of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?, bringing his signature humor to the show.


Early Life and Education

Jeremy Clarkson was born on April 11, 1960, in Doncaster, England. He attended Repton School, where he was known for his rebellious nature. After school, he pursued a career in journalism, specializing in automotive journalism.


Career

  • 1988–2015: Main presenter of BBC’s Top Gear
  • Since 2016: Presenter of Amazon’s The Grand Tour
  • Since 2018: Host of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?

Personal Life

Jeremy Clarkson has been married twice and has three children. He lives on a farm in the Cotswolds and has been running his own farm since 2021, documented in the series Clarkson’s Farm.
