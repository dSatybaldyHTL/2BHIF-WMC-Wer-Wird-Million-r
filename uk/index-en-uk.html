Introduction

Quiz Show

Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? is the quiz show where knowledge, strategy, and a bit of luck determine the path to a million.

Game Principle

With 15 questions and 4 lifelines, the contestant progresses step by step to the top.

Prize Money

Up to one million euros awaits the contestant. The prize money increases with each question, but at the same time, the questions become increasingly difficult.

About Us

  • September 2023

    Our Beginnings

    Since September 2023, we are five enthusiastic students from HTL Leonding in Austria, where we met and formed a good friendship.

  • July 2024

    Completion of First Year

    The first year was completely new and overwhelming with all the new subjects, but it taught us a lot about computer science. We learned the basics of the C# programming language and operating systems. Additionally, we were able to apply theoretical knowledge in practice in a subject called "WLC". In just one year, we learned more about computers and how they work than ever before. Fortunately, we all successfully completed the first year.

  • September 2024

    Beginning of Second Year

    After two months of rest and with the now acquired knowledge about computer science, we started the second year as Class 2BHIF. At the beginning of September, we initially spent time with organizational tasks before participating in a five-day sports week in Bibione, Italy.

  • October 2024

    Start of Our HTML/CSS Career

    Only in October did we have the great fortune to have the subject 'WMC' (Web and Mobile Computing). In this subject, we learned the basics of the HTML and CSS programming languages. Additionally, we were allowed to work in groups and choose a topic for which we should design a website. We chose the topic 'Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?' and have been working on this website since then.

    February 2025

    Semester certificate

    In February, we completed the first semester of the second year. It was challenging and overwhelming for many of us, but we have now got used to the new subjects, teachers and timetable. We are positive about the second semester and are convinced that we will improve our current grades by the end of the school year.
